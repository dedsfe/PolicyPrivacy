Subscription Terms

Last updated: January 2026

⚠️ Important Information

Please read carefully. Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled.

Options

PerfectEar Premium options:

  • Monthly: Full access for one month.

Prices may vary by region. Complete price details are displayed at confirmation of purchase.

What's Included

Premium includes:

  • Unlimited Lives
  • Access to All Instruments
  • Unlimited Practice Mode
  • Ad-Free Experience
  • Detailed Stats

Auto-Renewal Policy

  • Subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period.
  • Any unused portion of a free trial period, if offered, will be forfeited when purchasing a subscription.

Cancellation

To cancel:

  1. Open Settings on your device
  2. Tap your Name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select PerfectEar
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

You will retain access until the end of the paid period.

Payment

  • Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase.
  • All transactions are handled by Apple. We do not store payment details.
